Organized chaos!

It might sound like a bad thing, to be called chaotic, but I have turned it to my advantage.


As a designer, it made me think outside the box and it gave me edge. My chaotic side gives my creations a signature. A signature that not only makes my work uniqe, but also gives them life.
As a person, being chaotic made me creative and has given me a different way of thinking. I see patterns and solutions others often can't. I see the world differently.
And most important, as a programmer I am a problem solver all the way to my core. And the combination of problem solving and organized chaos makes me creative in my coding style, it makes my code a good combination of structured and inventive. I love finding new ways to solve problems and that is what makes programming my passion and future. It's constantly developing, pushing the limits further and the possibilites endless.
